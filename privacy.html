Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 5, 2025

ER Time ("we", "our", or "us") provides a healthcare utility app designed to help users locate nearby emergency rooms, view estimated wait times, and access directions. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect personal identifiers like your name, email address, or contact details. However, we may access the following types of data:

  • Location Data (Precise): We use your device’s precise location to help you find nearby emergency rooms and display directions on the map. This location is used temporarily and is not stored or shared.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we access is used exclusively for the following purpose:

  • To provide location-based results (e.g., hospitals near you).
  • To display routing and mapping features.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your location or any other user data with third parties.

4. Data Storage

We process your location data ephemerally — it is used in real-time to provide app features and is not retained or stored after use.

5. Account Information

No account is required to use this app. We do not collect usernames, passwords, or other identifiers.

6. Security

All data is transmitted securely using encryption in transit. We follow best practices to protect your information.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Your Rights

As no data is retained, there is no stored information to access, correct, or delete.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
