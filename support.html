üìö Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find nearby hospitals? ‚ñº

Use the "Nearby" tab in the app. The app will automatically detect your location and show hospitals within your selected radius. You can also search for specific hospitals or filter by specialty.

How accurate are the wait times? ‚ñº

Wait times are estimated using AI-powered predictions based on historical data, current conditions, and real-time reports from users. They are estimates and may vary.

Can I use the app offline? ‚ñº

Yes! The app uses hybrid storage, which means your data is saved locally. You can view previously loaded hospitals and your saved records even without an internet connection. Data will sync when you're back online.

How do I change my distance unit (miles/km)? ‚ñº

Go to Settings in the app and select your preferred distance unit. The change will apply immediately across all screens.

Is my data secure? ‚ñº

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and stored securely. You can request data export or account deletion at any time through the Privacy section.
