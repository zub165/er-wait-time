About ERTime101

ERTime101 helps patients and hospitals navigate emergency room visits more efficiently. The app provides estimated ER wait times, digital pre-registration, hospital location services, a medical chatbot, and patient feedback tools.

Patients can view nearby emergency rooms, register before arrival, and access maps with estimated travel times. Hospitals and clinics can improve triage, reduce paperwork, and gather service feedback to optimize care.

Important Disclaimer: All wait times shown in the app are estimates and may not reflect real-time conditions. Actual waiting may vary based on severity of cases, emergencies, or capacity. This app is for informational purposes only and is not a substitute for medical advice or emergency services. Always call 911 or your local emergency number in case of a life-threatening emergency.
