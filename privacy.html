Effective Date: September 18, 2025

ER Wait Time ("we", "our", or "us") provides a healthcare utility app designed to help users locate nearby emergency rooms, view estimated wait times, and access directions. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Data We Collect

We minimize data collection and do not collect personal identifiers like your name, email address, or contact details unless explicitly provided. However, we may access the following types of data:

  • Location Data (Precise): We use your device's precise location to help you find nearby emergency rooms and display directions on the map. This location is used temporarily and is not stored permanently.
  • App Usage Data: Basic usage statistics to improve app performance (non-personal).
  • Device Information: Basic device information for compatibility purposes (non-personal).

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we access is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To provide location-based results (e.g., hospitals near you).
  • To display routing and mapping features.
  • To improve app functionality and user experience.
  • To provide customer support when requested.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Limited sharing may occur with:

  • Map Service Providers: Google Maps or TomTom for location services
  • Analytics Services: Anonymous usage data for app improvement
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect user safety
